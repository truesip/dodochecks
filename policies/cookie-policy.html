Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Dodo Checks uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

2) Types of cookies we may use

  • Strictly necessary: Required for core site functionality and security.
  • Preferences: Help remember settings like language or region (if applicable).
  • Analytics: Help us understand traffic and usage trends to improve the website.
  • Marketing: Help measure marketing performance (if enabled).

3) Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings (for example, by blocking or deleting cookies). Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

4) Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” signal. Because there is no consistent industry standard for how to respond, we do not respond to such signals at this time.

5) Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above indicates when it was last revised.
