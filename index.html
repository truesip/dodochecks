How it works

A simple workflow that scales from small businesses to high-volume operations.

1) Send payment details

Upload a file or provide payment data from your system (and optional documents for enclosure).

2) Review and approve

Use internal approvals and permission controls to confirm what is being sent and by whom.

3) Print securely

Checks are printed in a controlled production process designed to reduce manual handling and errors.

4) Mail and track

We dispatch using supported mailing services and provide status updates for operational visibility.
