Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

This policy explains when refunds may be available for check printing and mailing services. Because physical production and postage are involved, refund availability depends on the order stage.

1) Cancellations before production

If you request cancellation before an order enters production (printing/enclosure), we may be able to cancel the order and issue a refund of applicable fees (excluding non-refundable transaction fees, if any).

2) Orders already in production or mailed

Once an order has entered production or has been mailed, we generally cannot offer a refund for printing and postage. This is because materials, production time, and carrier services have already been used.

3) Errors and reprints

If Dodo Checks makes a production error (for example, an incorrect print that does not match the data we received), we will work with you to correct the issue, which may include a reprint and/or remailing. If the issue was caused by data you supplied (incorrect payee, address, amount, or document), reprints may be subject to additional fees.

4) Returned mail and undeliverable items

If mail is returned as undeliverable, we can coordinate a reshipment after you provide an updated address. Additional printing and shipping fees may apply.

5) Chargebacks and disputes

If you have a billing concern, please contact us first so we can resolve it quickly. Unresolved disputes may be handled through your payment provider’s dispute process.

6) How to request a refund or cancellation

Email mailing@dodochecks.com with your order details. Please include your company name, order or reference ID (if available), and the reason for the request.

Tip: To maximize the chance of cancellation, contact us as soon as possible after submission.
