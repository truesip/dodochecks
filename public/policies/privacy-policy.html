Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dodo Checks ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website or use our services.

1) Scope

This policy applies to information we process in connection with the Service. It does not cover third parties we do not control (such as your bank, email provider, or shipping carrier).

2) Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Account and business information (e.g., name, company, role/title, phone number, email address, billing address).
  • Payment and billing information (e.g., invoice details and payment method metadata). If you pay by card or ACH, payment processing is typically handled by a third-party payment processor.
  • Check and remittance data you provide (e.g., payee name and address, payment amount, memo lines, remittance details, and associated documents you ask us to print or include).
  • Compliance and verification information (as needed) to help prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, or verify a business relationship.
  • Communications (e.g., emails or messages you send to us, support requests, and responses).
  • Usage and device information (e.g., IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, and timestamps).
  • Cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy.

3) How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Process requests to print and mail checks and associated documents.
  • Provide customer support, respond to inquiries, and send operational notices.
  • Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse of the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.
  • Send marketing communications (where permitted). You can opt out at any time.

4) How we share information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support, identity verification, and payment processing).
  • Printing and mailing partners and shipping carriers to produce and deliver items.
  • Professional advisors (e.g., legal, accounting) as needed.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when we believe disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers (e.g., a merger, acquisition, or asset sale), where information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary course of business.

5) Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and your relationship with us.

6) Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Your choices and rights

  • Marketing emails: You can opt out by following unsubscribe instructions in the email.
  • Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy.
  • Access, correction, deletion: Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

8) Children’s privacy

The Service is intended for business use and not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above indicates when this policy was last revised.

10) Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at mailing@dodochecks.com or write to: 1209 Mountain Road PL NE, STE R, Albuquerque, NM 87110, USA.
